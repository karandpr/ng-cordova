Cordova with the power of AngularJS

ngCordova was a collection of 70+ AngularJS extensions on top of the Cordova API that make it easy to build, test, and deploy Cordova mobile apps with AngularJS. This is a fork of original ng-cordova. Some of my projects use ng-cordova extensively. It may or may not be feasible to move them to ionic-native. I would be maintaining this thing till I can.
